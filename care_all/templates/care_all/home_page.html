Our Services

What we do

We offer support, financial and non, as well as training and education aimed at creating opportunities for better lives. One life at a time. We support various causes all over the world, our actions; some are indiviuals, some collectives. Some need financial support only, others need training, education, sometimes guidance. We tell their stories and through your generous contributions, offer them the one going or one time support they need.

Where we do it

Care-All Foundation operates worldwide, with offices based in Spain. We are a registered foundation and abide by all spanish laws, rules and regulations. Our actions are located all over the globe. Our donors come from all over the world. Our team is of varied nationalities. There are no geographical boundries to kindness, it is far reaching. Our approach is personalized, human. We interact personally with all those we help, they become friends, sources of inspiration to us.

How to help

Want your donation to go where it is most needed? Donate to our general fund.

When you make a gift, you will instantly receive an email with a profile of a family whose lives are being transformed by our programs.
